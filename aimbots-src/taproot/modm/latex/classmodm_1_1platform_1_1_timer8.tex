\item TRG\+\_\+\+COM (used by INTERRUPT\+\_\+\+TRIGGER and INTERRUPT\+\_\+\+COM)
\item CC (used by INTERRUPT\+\_\+\+CAPTURE\+\_\+\+COMPARE\+\_\+1..3)
